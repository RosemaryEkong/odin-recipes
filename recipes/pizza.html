PIZZA

DESCRIPTION

Pizza is a dish of Italian origin consisting of a usually round, flat base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomatoes, cheese, and often various other ingredients, which is then baked at a high temperature, traditionally in a wood-fired oven. A small pizza is sometimes called a pizzetta.

INGREDIENTS

  • Cheese
  • Flour
  • Tomato
  • Olive oil
  • mozzarella
  • Basil
  • Pizza dough

STEPS

  1. Gather Ingredients. Heat the sauce while chopping up these ingredients...
  2. Prepare the Dough. This step takes some practice.
  3. Add the Toppings. I like to layer the cheese then pepperoni last.. with all other ingredients underneath it.
  4. Heat and Then Enjoy.
